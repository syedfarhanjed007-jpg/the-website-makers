Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2024

At The Website Makers, we strive to deliver the highest quality services. We understand that circumstances may change, and you may need to cancel a service. This policy outlines our cancellation and refund procedures.

1. Cancellation Policy

You may request cancellation of a service or project under the following conditions:

  • Before Project Commencement: Full cancellation is possible if requested before any work has started. A nominal administrative fee of up to 10% of the advance payment may be deducted.
  • During Design Phase: If the project is in the design/research phase, cancellation is possible with charges for work completed up to that point.
  • During Development Phase: Once development work has begun, cancellation charges will include all work completed plus any third-party costs incurred.
  • After Delivery: Cancellation is not applicable after the final deliverables have been handed over and accepted by the client.

All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email to connect@thewebsitemakers.in.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds are processed based on the following conditions:

  • Eligible Refund: If cancellation is requested before work begins, a refund of up to 90% of the advance payment will be issued.
  • Partial Refund: If work has partially commenced, a refund will be calculated based on the percentage of work remaining, minus any third-party costs already incurred.
  • No Refund: No refund will be issued if the project has been completed and delivered, or if the client has approved the final deliverables.
  • Monthly Services: For ongoing monthly services (SEO, digital marketing), no refund is provided for the current billing month. Cancellation takes effect from the next billing cycle.

Refunds do not apply to domain registration, hosting fees, or third-party software licenses purchased on behalf of the client.

3. Processing Time

Once a refund is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days from the date of approval
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used during the transaction
  • Bank processing times may vary and are beyond our control
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated
